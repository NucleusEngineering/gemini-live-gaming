The Premise
Simba is a battle-hardened, street-smart tabby and former operative of the Feline Intelligence Agency (FIA). He left that life behind until his best friend, a laid-back orange tabby named Omar, was abducted by the ruthless K-9 Syndicate. Omar stumbled upon their plans for the "Ultimate Vacuum"—a weapon of mass terror designed to rid the neighborhood of cats forever. Armed with his trusty Hairball Cannon and Laser-Sight Rifle, Simba must shoot his way through enemy lines to get his buddy back.

Level 1: The Backyard Trenches
Setting: A sprawling suburban backyard converted into a heavily fortified military outpost.

Plot: Simba is dropped in by a crow ally. He must breach the K-9 Syndicate's perimeter defenses to find the entrance to their underground bunker.

Gameplay Focus: Cover-based shooting and introduction to mechanics. Simba fights low-ranking Terrier grunts and evades motion-sensing lawn sprinklers.

Objective: Hack the electronic doggy door to gain access to the main facility.

Level 2: The Grooming Facility
Setting: A terrifying, sterile laboratory where cats are subjected to forced baths and ridiculous haircuts.

Plot: Simba tracks Omar’s scent to this torture chamber, only to find he was just moved to a higher security sector. Simba must destroy the water-pump generators to flood the enemy's comms room.

Gameplay Focus: Fast-paced movement on slippery tile floors. Environmental hazards include blast-valves of warm soapy water. Enemies are Hazmat-suited Poodles wielding blow-dryer flamethrowers.

Objective: Sabotage the main water lines and retrieve the red keycard from the Grooming Overseer.

Level 3: The Ventilation Labyrinth
Setting: The dark, claustrophobic air ducts running between the facility's walls.

Plot: To bypass the heavily guarded main hallways, Simba takes to the vents. He discovers the Syndicate has allied with the Cyber-Rat cartel to patrol the tight spaces.

Gameplay Focus: Stealth and close-quarters combat. Simba uses night vision and suppressed weapons.

Objective: Survive the ambush of exploding Cyber-Rats and drop down securely into the industrial zone.

Level 4: The Kibble Processing Plant
Setting: A massive, industrial factory with conveyer belts of dry dog food and giant grinding gears.

Plot: Simba intercepts a radio transmission confirming Omar is being held in the Penthouse. To get there, he must cross the factory floor and hijack the freight elevator.

Gameplay Focus: Verticality and heavy combat. Navigating moving platforms while dodging sniper fire. Enemies are Rottweiler Heavies with Gatling guns that shoot reinforced kibble.

Objective: Defeat the floor guards, overload the kibble vats to cause a distraction, and ride the elevator up.

Level 5: The Penthouse Kennel & Final Boss
Setting: A luxurious, high-tech command center overlooking the entire neighborhood, featuring plush dog beds and chew-toy armories.

Plot: Simba breaches the penthouse to find Omar trapped in an impenetrable laser-cage. Standing in his way is the mastermind behind the K-9 Syndicate.

The Boss: General Iron-Jaw, a massive, scarred Bullmastiff wearing a heavily armored mech-suit.

Boss Fight Mechanics:

Phase 1: Iron-Jaw fires barrages from his shoulder-mounted Tennis Ball Mortars. Simba must use agility to dodge and shoot the weak joints on his mech-legs.

Phase 2: Iron-Jaw deploys autonomous Vacuum Drones. Simba has to shoot the drones so they explode near the General to break his energy shields.

Phase 3: Iron-Jaw resorts to his devastating Sonic Bark. Simba must use the environment for heavy cover, landing precise headshots until the mech-suit is destroyed.

Objective: Defeat General Iron-Jaw, smash the cage control panel, and extract with Omar via a helicopter flown by allied birds.
